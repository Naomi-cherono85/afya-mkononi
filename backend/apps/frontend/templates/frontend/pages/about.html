About Afya Mkononi

Your AI-powered health companion

Afya Mkononi is an AI-powered health assistant that helps you ask general health questions, book clinic appointments, and stay on top of medications and follow-ups — all from one place. It is built to make routine support easier, not to replace your doctor.

How it works

1

Ask

Chat with the assistant about general health topics — symptoms, prevention, medication basics, when to see a clinician.

2

Book

Schedule an appointment with your clinic. Pick a date and time, share the reason, and the clinic takes it from there.

3

Remind

Set reminders for medications, follow-up visits, or anything else you don't want to forget. We'll surface them at the right time.

What Afya Mkononi will not do

These boundaries are built into the assistant by design.

  • No diagnoses

    The assistant never tells you what condition you have. Only a clinician can do that, in person.

  • No prescriptions

    The assistant will not tell you to start, stop, or change a medication. Always check with a licensed provider.

  • No replacement for professional care

    Afya Mkononi provides general information only. If your situation is serious or unclear, talk to a clinician.

If it is an emergency, call for help first

Do not use Afya Mkononi for emergencies. Call your local emergency number or go to the nearest emergency department right away if you have any of these:

  • Severe chest pain
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Severe bleeding
  • Loss of consciousness
  • Stroke-like symptoms (face drooping, arm weakness, slurred speech)
  • Severe allergic reaction
  • Pregnancy danger signs
