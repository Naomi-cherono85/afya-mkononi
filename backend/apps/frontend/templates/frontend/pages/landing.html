{# ----- Safety / trust ----- #}

Built with safe boundaries

Afya Mkononi is designed to support you responsibly. These limits are built into the assistant by design — so you always know where AI guidance ends and professional care begins.

Learn how it works
  • No diagnoses

    The assistant never tells you what condition you have — only a clinician can do that, in person.

  • No prescriptions

    It will never tell you to start, stop, or change a medication. Always check with a licensed provider.

  • No replacement for care

    General information only. If your situation is serious or unclear, talk to a clinician right away.
